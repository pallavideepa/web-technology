KARNATAKA TOURISM

Karnataka, the sixth largest state in India, has been ranked as the third most popular state in the country for tourism in 2014. is home to 507 of the 3600 centrally protected monuments in India, second only to Uttar Pradesh. The State Directorate of Archaeology and Museums protects an additional 752 monuments and another 25,000 monuments are yet to receive protection.
